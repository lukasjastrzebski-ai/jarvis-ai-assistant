## App Description

### Short Description (170 characters)
Meet Jarvis - your AI-powered personal assistant that unifies your emails, calendar, and tasks into one intelligent inbox with voice commands and smart scheduling.

### Full Description (4000 characters max)

Jarvis is your intelligent personal assistant that brings together all your communication and scheduling needs into one unified experience. Powered by advanced AI, Jarvis learns your preferences and helps you stay organized effortlessly.

**UNIFIED INBOX**
- See all your emails, calendar events, and tasks in one place
- Smart filtering automatically prioritizes what matters most
- Quick actions let you respond, schedule, or delegate with a tap

**SMART CALENDAR INTEGRATION**
- Seamlessly connect Google Calendar and Apple Calendar
- AI-powered scheduling finds the best times for your meetings
- Daily planning generates personalized schedules based on your priorities

**VOICE COMMANDS**
- "Hey Jarvis" - activate with just your voice
- Natural language processing understands what you mean
- Dictate emails, create events, and manage tasks hands-free

**AI-POWERED ASSISTANCE**
- Smart email drafting suggests professional responses
- Meeting preparation with context from your calendar and emails
- Learns your preferences to provide personalized recommendations

**MEMORY SYSTEM**
- Jarvis remembers important details about your contacts
- Contextual awareness improves over time
- Review and manage what Jarvis knows in the Memory Bank

**ACTIVITY LOG**
- Full transparency into every action
- See what Jarvis has done on your behalf
- Review, approve, or modify any automated actions

**PRIVACY FIRST**
- Your data stays on your device with local-first architecture
- Optional cloud sync with end-to-end encryption
- No selling of personal data - ever

Transform the way you manage your digital life. Download Jarvis today and experience the future of personal productivity.
