# Jarvis AI Assistant - Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** January 16, 2026
**Last Updated:** January 16, 2026

## Introduction

Jarvis AI Assistant ("Jarvis", "we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using Jarvis, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

---

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

**Account Information**
- Email address (for account creation and authentication)
- Display name (optional)

**Calendar Data**
- Calendar events from connected accounts (Google Calendar, Apple Calendar)
- Event titles, dates, times, locations, and attendees

**Email Data**
- Email messages from connected accounts (Gmail)
- Sender information, subjects, and message content

**Voice Data**
- Voice commands when you use the voice interaction feature
- Voice data is processed locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers

**Memory Data**
- Preferences and facts you explicitly share with Jarvis
- Information Jarvis learns from your interactions

### Information Collected Automatically

**Usage Data**
- App feature usage patterns
- Error logs and crash reports
- Performance metrics

**Device Information**
- Device type and operating system version
- Unique device identifiers

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

1. **Provide Core Functionality**
   - Display your emails, calendar events, and tasks
   - Generate smart schedules and recommendations
   - Process voice commands

2. **Personalize Your Experience**
   - Learn your preferences over time
   - Provide relevant suggestions and drafts

3. **Improve Our Services**
   - Analyze usage patterns to improve features
   - Diagnose technical issues

4. **Communicate With You**
   - Send important service updates
   - Respond to support requests

## Data Storage and Security

### Local-First Architecture

Jarvis uses a local-first architecture, meaning:
- Your data is primarily stored on your device
- You have full control over your data
- The app works offline with locally cached data

### Cloud Sync (Optional)

If you enable cloud sync:
- Data is encrypted end-to-end
- Only you can decrypt your data
- We cannot read your synced content

### Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures:
- TLS encryption for all data transmission
- Secure authentication protocols
- Regular security audits
