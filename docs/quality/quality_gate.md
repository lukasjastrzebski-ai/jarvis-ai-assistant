# Quality Gate

The Quality Gate determines whether execution may continue.

---

## Gate inputs

- Test results
- Execution reports
- Signal snapshots (if applicable)

---

## Pass criteria

Execution may continue if:
- all required tests pass
- no known regressions exist
- acceptance criteria are met
- quality baseline is respected

---

## Fail criteria

Execution must STOP if:
- critical tests fail
- regressions are detected
- reports are incomplete
- quality baseline is violated

---

## Authority

The Product Owner makes the final decision, informed by evidence.
