# Product Owner Override

This document defines how manual PO signals override automated recommendations.

---

## When to use override
- strategic pivots
- customer commitments
- legal or compliance issues
- existential business risks

---

## Override rules
- Overrides must be explicit and documented
- Rationale must be written
- Overrides may bypass decision engine ranking

---

## Documentation
Record overrides in:
- docs/signals/po_override.md
- link to impacted decisions or tasks

Manual authority is final.
